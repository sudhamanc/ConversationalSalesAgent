- **Guaranteed Uptime**: 99.9% monthly uptime

- **Service Credits**: Pro-rated monthly fee credits for downtime exceeding 99.9%

### Credit Policy
| Uptime % | Service Credit |
|----------|----------------|
| 99.9% - 99.0% | 10% of monthly fee |
| 98.9% - 97.0% | 25% of monthly fee |
| < 97.0% | 50% of monthly fee |

### Exclusions
- Scheduled maintenance (with 72-hour notice)
- Force majeure events
- Customer equipment failures
- Issues within customer premises

## Installation

### Installation Process
1. **Site Survey**: Technician assesses fiber connection point
2. **Fiber Installation**: Run fiber cable from street to premises
3. **ONT Installation**: Install and configure Optical Network Terminal
4. **Gateway Setup**: Configure Business Gateway router
5. **Testing**: Verify speeds and connectivity
6. **Training**: Brief customer on equipment usage

### Installation Timeline
- **Standard Installation**: 5-7 business days from order
- **Express Installation**: 2-3 business days (additional $99 fee)
- **Installation Fee**: Included (waived for 12+ month contracts)

### Installation Requirements
- Fiber availability at address (check with Serviceability Agent)
- Power outlet near installation point
- Access to premises for technician
- 2-4 hour installation window

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the upload speed really the same as download?**  
A: Yes! Fiber 1G provides true symmetrical speeds - 1 Gbps download AND 1 Gbps upload.

**Q: Can I upgrade to a higher speed tier later?**  
A: Yes, you can upgrade to Fiber 5G or 10G at any time. Contact support for details.

**Q: What happens if I exceed 99.9% uptime threshold?**  
A: You'll automatically receive service credits on your next bill per the SLA table above.

**Q: Can I use my own router instead of the included gateway?**  
A: Yes, you can use your own router, but our gateway must remain connected to the fiber ONT.

**Q: Is there a data cap?**  
A: No, Business Fiber 1G has unlimited data with no caps or throttling.
