# Business Mobile Products — Business Knowledge Base

## Overview

Our Business Mobile portfolio provides 4G LTE and 5G mobile service designed specifically for business accounts. All plans include unlimited talk and text, business account management portals, and device financing options. The three tiers differ in data speed, coverage network (4G vs. 5G vs. 5G Ultra Wideband), hotspot allowance, and international capabilities.

Business Mobile lines can be bundled with our internet and voice products for consolidated billing and management.

**Product IDs covered:** MOB-BAS, MOB-UNL, MOB-PREM

---

## Business Mobile Basic (MOB-BAS)

### Description
Cost-effective business mobile plan for teams that primarily use mobile phones for calls, texts, and light data usage. 10 GB of high-speed data per line per month is sufficient for email, messaging apps, map navigation, and occasional web browsing. Overage is throttled to 600 Kbps rather than charged additional fees.

### Technical Specifications
- **Data:** 10 GB high-speed data per line per month
- **Network:** 4G LTE Nationwide
- **Hotspot:** 5 GB mobile hotspot per month (shared from data allotment)
- **Data after 10 GB:** Throttled to 600 Kbps (no overage charges)
- **Talk:** Unlimited
- **Text:** Unlimited (US)

### Features
- 10 GB high-speed data per line
- Unlimited talk and text
- 4G LTE nationwide coverage
- Mobile hotspot (5 GB, counted from data allotment)
- Business account management portal (add/remove lines, view usage, pay bill)
- Device financing available (0% interest over 24 months for approved credit)

### Coverage
4G LTE nationwide coverage reaches approximately 99% of the US population. Outside major metropolitan areas, speeds vary by tower density. Rural coverage is available but at lower throughput.

### Use Cases — Best Fit
- Field service technicians that use mobile primarily for calls and work order apps
- Delivery drivers with route apps and dispatch communications
- Retail associates who primarily use devices for in-store operations
- Cost-conscious teams where most work is done on Wi-Fi (office or home) and mobile is supplemental
- Lines that are primarily backup or rarely used (e.g., a shared tablet in a service vehicle)

### Limitations
- High-definition video streaming will quickly consume the 10 GB allotment
- Not recommended for lines that frequently work in areas without Wi-Fi
- No 5G access; if 5G is a requirement, upgrade to MOB-UNL or MOB-PREM

## Business Mobile Unlimited (MOB-UNL)

### Description
Unlimited 5G business mobile for teams that require reliable, always-on connectivity regardless of data volume. Access to the 5G Nationwide network provides faster speeds than 4G LTE in most urban and suburban coverage areas. Includes 25 GB hotspot, priority network access, and international texting.

### Technical Specifications
- **Data:** Unlimited high-speed 5G data
- **Network:** 5G Nationwide (with 4G LTE fallback where 5G is not available)
- **Hotspot:** 25 GB high-speed mobile hotspot per month; throttled to 600 Kbps after 25 GB
- **Talk:** Unlimited
- **Text:** Unlimited (US) + International texting to 200+ countries at no additional charge
- **Priority:** Business priority on congested towers (ahead of consumer unlimited lines)

### Features
- Unlimited 5G data (deprioritized during network congestion after 50 GB/month per line)
- Unlimited talk and text
- 5G Nationwide coverage (sub-6 GHz 5G)
- Mobile hotspot (25 GB high-speed)
- International texting included (200+ countries)
- Business account management portal
- Device financing available
- Priority network access on congested cells

### 5G Nationwide Coverage
5G Nationwide uses sub-6 GHz spectrum (primarily 600 MHz and 2.5 GHz bands). This provides broad geographic coverage — available in most urban, suburban, and growing rural areas — at speeds typically 20–50% faster than 4G LTE on the same device. 5G Nationwide is not the same as 5G Ultra Wideband (mmWave), which is available on MOB-PREM.

### Use Cases — Best Fit
- Sales professionals who work primarily on the road or in the field
- Healthcare workers accessing EHR systems and medical imaging apps from mobile devices
- Executives who need reliable connectivity everywhere without thinking about data caps
- Teams frequently using mobile as a hotspot for laptop connectivity in the field
- Outside sales teams with CRM apps, video conferencing, and large file access on mobile

### Common Customer Questions
**Q: What is "deprioritization" and when does it affect me?**
A: After a line uses 50 GB of data in a month, the carrier may temporarily slow that line's speeds if the tower it's connected to is congested (many users active simultaneously). In practice, this affects a small percentage of customers and only during peak congestion windows. Business lines receive priority access ahead of consumer accounts at the same congestion level.

**Q: Is 5G Nationwide available everywhere?**
A: 5G Nationwide is available across most of the continental US. Coverage maps are available on our portal. For areas with limited 5G, the device automatically falls back to 4G LTE.

## Business Mobile Premium (MOB-PREM)

### Description
Ultra-premium 5G business mobile for executives and power users. Access to 5G Ultra Wideband (the fastest, highest-capacity 5G network tier using mmWave and mid-band spectrum in dense urban areas) along with 50 GB hotspot, international roaming in 30+ countries, smartwatch connectivity, and MDM integration. Designed for the most demanding mobile use cases.

### Technical Specifications
- **Data:** Unlimited premium 5G data (Ultra Wideband where available)
- **Network:** 5G Ultra Wideband (mmWave + C-band) in covered areas; 5G Nationwide + 4G LTE everywhere else
- **Hotspot:** 50 GB high-speed mobile hotspot per month (throttled to 1 Mbps after 50 GB — not 600 Kbps)
- **Talk:** Unlimited
- **Text:** Unlimited (US) + International texting
- **Roaming:** International voice and data roaming in 30+ countries (standard international rates apply; pass available)
- **Priority:** Highest business priority classification on congested towers

### Features
- Unlimited premium 5G Ultra Wideband data
- 50 GB high-speed mobile hotspot
- International: roaming in 30+ countries (Europe, Canada, Mexico, select Asia-Pacific)
- Apple Watch and smartwatch cellular connectivity (1 device per line, included at no charge)
- Premium device trade-in program (higher trade-in values than lower tiers)
- Business account management portal
- Mobile Device Management (MDM) integration support (Microsoft Intune, Jamf Pro, VMware Workspace ONE)
- Dedicated business support line (priority queue)
- Device financing available

### 5G Ultra Wideband Coverage
5G Ultra Wideband uses mmWave (millimeter wave, 24–47 GHz) and C-band (3.7–3.98 GHz) spectrum. This delivers:
- **mmWave speeds:** 1–4 Gbps in dense urban locations (stadiums, airports, convention centers, downtown cores)
- **C-band speeds:** 200–500 Mbps in urban and suburban areas

Ultra Wideband coverage is available in major metro areas. Coverage maps are available via the business portal. Outside UW coverage, devices fall back to 5G Nationwide or 4G LTE automatically.

### MDM Integration
Premium lines support enterprise Mobile Device Management integration out of the box:
- Supports **Microsoft Intune**, **Jamf Pro**, and **VMware Workspace ONE**
- Enables remote wipe, app policy enforcement, and conditional access (device must be enrolled in MDM to access corporate apps)
- BYOD and corporate-owned device scenarios both supported
- SIM lock/unlock and eSIM provisioning managed through the business portal with MDM integration

### International Roaming
Standard roaming is available in 30+ countries with the Premium plan:
- **Included regions:** Canada, Mexico, EU countries, UK, select Asia-Pacific
- Voice roaming: standard per-minute rates (or included with an International Day Pass add-on)
- Data roaming: tiered speeds depending on country; LTE available in most covered countries
- For frequent international travelers, an **International Day Pass** add-on ($10/day, pause when not activated) is available via the account portal

### Use Cases — Best Fit
- C-suite executives (CEO, CTO, CFO) requiring the best mobile experience
- Sales directors and VPs frequently traveling internationally
- Mobile-heavy professionals who use mobile as a primary laptop hotspot
- IT administrators managing corporate mobile devices via MDM
- Organizations attending trade shows, events, or operating in airports/stadiums where Ultra Wideband provides maximum speeds

## Business Mobile Bundling Notes

Business Mobile lines can be combined with internet and voice products on a single business account:
- Single monthly invoice
- Consolidated business portal for all services
- Bundle discounts may be available — route pricing inquiries to the OfferManagement agent
- Mobile can serve as a secondary failover WAN link when combined with our Business Routers (requires supported gateway model)

## Device Policy
We do not sell mobile devices directly — customers bring their own compatible unlocked devices or finance devices through our device partner program. Premium trade-in credits are highest on the MOB-PREM tier. Unlocked devices must support the correct band frequencies for US networks.
